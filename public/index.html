The Bright Side of Automation

How the Media Portrays Robots

In this Video clip, we see two famous scenes from Terminator, and 2001: A Space Odessey. These two clips show two common fears people have around automation and robots.


Common Fears about Automation

  • Robots aren't safe or trustworthy
  • Automation will take all of our jobs
  • Robots will be come Sentient and start the apocalypse

The Goal of this Website

This website was made to show that robots are beneficial to society and that these common fears have no basis other than media craze. Robotics and Automation are going to become more prevalent in society as technologies and methods are developed. Knowing how Automation works and why robots are here to help is important knowledge for everyone.
